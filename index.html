FAQ

Q: Why does MindBlock need Screen Time / Family Controls permission?
A: MindBlock uses Apple's Screen Time APIs to temporarily hide the apps you selected, so you can stay focused when needed. The app does not access or upload your screen time statistics or browsing history.

Q: Why does it need "Always Allow" location permission?
A: When you enable location-based rules (for example, blocking entertainment apps at work), the system needs to detect when you enter or leave specific regions in the background. Location data is only used locally for geofence evaluation and is never uploaded or stored as history.

Q: Is any of my data uploaded?
A: No. All configuration data (app groups, schedules, location rules, etc.) is stored locally on your device. We do not run any servers and do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
